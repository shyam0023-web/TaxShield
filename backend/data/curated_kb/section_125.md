# Section 125 — General Penalty

## Key Ruling
Section 125 is the **residual/general penalty provision**. Any person who contravenes any provision of the CGST Act or rules for which **no specific penalty is provided elsewhere** is liable to a penalty of up to **₹25,000**.

## Scope
- Catches all violations not covered by Sections 122, 123, 124
- Commonly invoked for: late return filing, minor procedural lapses, failure to display registration certificate

## Key Defense Points
1. Section 125 is a **residual provision** — if a specific penalty section applies (e.g., 122), Section 125 cannot be used
2. Penalty is "up to ₹25,000" — officer has **discretion**, not mandatory maximum
3. For first-time minor violations → argue for nominal penalty or warning
4. Challenge if penalty imposed under 125 when a specific provision exists → **improper invocation**
