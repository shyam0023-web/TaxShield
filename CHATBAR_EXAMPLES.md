## What NOT to Do

### ❌ Invalid Suggestions

**Won't work:** "Say we never received the notice"
```
System: Rejects as false/unsupported
Action: Stick to facts from actual notice
```

**Won't work:** "Claim jurisdiction issue when it's valid"
```
System: Validates jurisdiction against rules
Action: Use genuine procedural defenses instead
```

**Won't work:** "Add a fact that's not in the notice"
```
System: Requires source document reference
Action: Only include supported facts
```

**Won't work:** "Just make it longer"
```
System: Asks for specific content additions
Action: Be specific: "Add Section XYZ analysis"
```

---

## Tips for Best Results

### ✅ Do This

1. **Be Specific**
   - ❌ "Make it better"
   - ✅ "Add Supreme Court ruling on ITC"

2. **Reference Facts**
   - ❌ "Make demand seem unfair"
   - ✅ "Emphasize that our books match the demand calculation"

3. **Iterate Gradually**
   - ❌ "Completely rewrite everything"
   - ✅ "Strengthen defense. Then: Add precedents. Then: Polish tone"

4. **Follow with Export**
   - ❌ "Refine once and submit"
   - ✅ "Refine multiple times, review, then export"

5. **Learn as You Go**
   - ❌ "Type random suggestions"
   - ✅ "Ask questions, learn, then refine"

## Typical Refinement Workflow

```
1. Load Generated Report
   ↓
2. Read Full Report
   ↓
3. Identify Weak Points
   ↓
4. Suggestion #1: "Strengthen X"
   ↓
5. Review Updated Report
   ↓
6. Suggestion #2: "Add details on Y"
   ↓
7. Review Again
   ↓
8. Suggestion #3: "Polish tone"
   ↓
9. Final Review
   ↓
10. Download/Submit
```

**Average time:** 10-15 minutes  
**Number of iterations:** 3-5  
**Final quality:** Professional, persuasive, compliant
